About

Grand Vibe City RP is a serious SA-MP roleplay server focused on realism.

We offer custom systems, factions, businesses, and balanced economy.

Our staff ensures fair, immersive, and respectful roleplay.

Roleplay Rules

General Conduct

  • Respect all players and staff.
  • No harassment, hate speech, or toxic behavior.
  • Follow staff instructions at all times.
  • Bug abuse or exploiting is strictly prohibited.
  • Cheating or unfair modifications result in permanent bans.

Roleplay Standards

  • You must stay in character (IC) at all times.
  • Metagaming (using OOC info IC) is not allowed.
  • Powergaming or unrealistic actions are forbidden.
  • Always value your character's life.
  • FearRP must be followed in dangerous situations.

Combat & Violence

  • Random Deathmatch (RDM) and Vehicle Deathmatch (VDM) are prohibited.
  • Proper roleplay initiation is required before violence.
  • No kill-on-sight unless explicitly permitted.
  • Revenge killing without RP justification is not allowed.

Punishments

  • Punishments include warnings, jail, temporary bans, or permanent bans.
  • Repeated rule violations lead to harsher penalties.
  • Ignorance of rules is not an excuse.

Server Features

  • Realistic job system (Taxi, Trucker, Mechanic, Lawyer, Reporter, Fisherman, etc.)
  • Advanced business system with ownership and income
  • Balanced economy with realistic prices
  • Custom vehicles and mapping
  • Interactive interiors and roleplay commands
  • Anti-cheat and admin monitoring systems
  • Frequent updates and community-driven features

Faction Rules

Police Department

  • Officers must follow chain of command.
  • Use force only when roleplay-justified.
  • No corruption without admin approval.
  • Professional behavior is mandatory.

EMS / Medical Services

  • Always prioritize saving lives.
  • No refusal of emergency roleplay.
  • Maintain realistic medical roleplay.

Criminal Gangs

  • Gang actions must have valid roleplay reasons.
  • No random shootings or kidnappings.
  • Gang wars require admin approval.
  • Respect FearRP at all times.
